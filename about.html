Tucker Act


After his son was murdered by the mother's boyfriend, after the years my father font to get us out of that house the money spent the lawyers, he decided that he was never going to let this happen to another person again. It took him three years after my brother died to pass the Tucker Act. this act states that Child Protective Services must perform surprise visits rather than scheduling it with the accused abuser as they had been before. This was to prevent the abuser from coaching the family ahead of time. And also that the parent did not have to be present like it was before but instead could be a school counselor. In the year following the Tucker act deaths in cases resulting in child abuse went from 32 to 8 in the state of Kentucky and they continued to decline and remain low thanks to my brother and my father. We are Family

My father says the most appealing thing that he does is helping others. He wants to keep other families together he wants to build a safe community. He intends this to be his legacy. My father is the oldest person to graduate the Kentucky police Academy at the age of 44. Because he's always wanted to do it and he got out of the rut he was in and decided to go for it. He started out as a DARE officer at the school in which he was able to impact children's lives everyday. He is often quoted as saying we are family and I will still here “his kids” use that catch phrase when we're in public. He was elected sheriff only four years ago beating out the incumbent sheriff of 21 years. He wanted things to change and he took it upon himself to change it and that quality is something that I will cherish as he is passed on to me.
